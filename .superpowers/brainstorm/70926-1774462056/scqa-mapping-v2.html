How visibly should SCQA be surfaced to the visitor?

The logic is correct either way — this is about whether the visitor sees the framework or just feels it.

A

Invisible — SCQA as Logic Only

Section labels stay as "THE PROFILE", "THE DIAGNOSIS", "THE ARCHITECTURE", "THE SHIFT". SCQA structures the narrative internally but is never named. The visitor feels the logic without seeing the framework. Consistent with the site's no-jargon principle.

B

Dual Label — SCQA + Curiosity Label

Each section shows both: a small monospace "SITUATION" above the Pink section label. SCQA gives visitors with a consulting background an immediate cognitive anchor. The Curiosity Loop label makes the methodology proprietary. Shows the framework AND the thinking.

C

SCQA as Primary Label

Replace the Curiosity Loop act labels entirely. "SITUATION.", "COMPLICATION.", "QUESTION.", "ANSWER." Clean, confident, universally legible to anyone with a consulting or business background. No explanation needed.
