How visibly should SCQA be surfaced?

The logic is correct either way — this is about whether the visitor sees the framework or just feels it.

A

Invisible — SCQA as Logic Only

Section labels stay as "THE PROFILE", "THE DIAGNOSIS", "THE ARCHITECTURE", "THE SHIFT". SCQA structures the narrative internally but is never named. The visitor feels the logic without seeing the framework. Consistent with the site's "no jargon" principle.

B

Dual Label — SCQA + Curiosity Label

Each section shows both: SITUATION —— above the Pink Curiosity label. SCQA gives the visitor a cognitive anchor they may already know. The Curiosity Loop label makes it proprietary. Shows the framework AND the methodology.

C

SCQA as Primary Label

Replace the Curiosity Loop act labels entirely. "SITUATION.", "COMPLICATION.", "QUESTION.", "ANSWER." in JetBrains Mono. Universally legible to anyone with a consulting background. Clean, confident, no explanation needed.
