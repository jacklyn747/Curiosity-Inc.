WebGL Hero — Full Design

React Three Fiber · GSAP scroll scrub · 800 particles · Mobile SVG fallback

1. What the visitor sees

On load — Dark void. 800 white particles drifting slowly in random positions. No text. The ground breathes.
First scroll — Particles begin converging. As you scroll down, they lock into a Fibonacci spiral, then unfold into the Flower of Life ring pattern.
Mid scroll — Geometry tightens into a radial sphere cluster. The Teal→Orange→Pink gradient bleeds across the particle colors at the moment of convergence.
Scroll completes — Two lines of text fade in over the geometry:
"Your audience is learning from you."
"You just haven't designed what they're learning."
Continue scrolling — Hero section exits, Section 1 (The Scaffold) enters normally.

2. Scroll map (GSAP ScrollTrigger scrub)

0% ──── Idle drift (ambient only, no scroll dependency)
0–30% ─── Scattered → Fibonacci spiral
30–60% ── Fibonacci → Flower of Life
60–85% ── Flower of Life → Sphere cluster + color gradient fires
85–100% ─ Text lines fade in (opacity 0→1, staggered 300ms)
Hero section is pinned (position:sticky) during the full scroll sequence (~300vh total scroll distance)

3. Particle system

Count: 800 particles on desktop, 300 on mobile WebGL (if ever used)
Geometry: Three.js BufferGeometry with position attribute — 4 pre-computed target arrays, each 800×3 floats
Material: PointsMaterial, size 1.5px, white at 70% opacity. Particle color shifts to Teal→Orange→Pink during sphere stage via vertexColors
Idle drift: Each particle has a random phase offset. A useFrame loop adds a slow sine-wave displacement (amplitude 0.3 units) — imperceptible as structure, just life
Morph: GSAP tweens the position BufferAttribute values between target arrays. invalidateFrameloop ensures r3f only renders when something changes

4. Mobile fallback

Triggered when: window.matchMedia('(pointer: coarse)') or WebGL context creation fails
Shows: Static SVG Flower of Life (~12 overlapping circles, Teal strokes, 0.5px, 30% opacity) — drawn via stroke-dashoffset on mount
Text appears after SVG draw completes (~1.2s). Same two lines, same stagger. No scroll dependency.
Matches the project's existing SVG animation vocabulary exactly.

5. Performance guardrails

AdaptiveDpr from drei — scales canvas pixel ratio down automatically if FPS drops below 30
frameloop="demand" on Canvas — r3f only renders on scroll or idle drift tick, not every frame
Pre-computed geometry targets (calculated once on mount, not per frame)
Hero unmounts the r3f canvas once it scrolls fully out of view (IntersectionObserver)
