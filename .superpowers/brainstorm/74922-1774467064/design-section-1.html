Design — Part 1 of 4: Component Architecture

How the hero fits into the existing codebase

New Files

src/components/hero/
HeroSection.tsx — Top-level component. Renders the canvas + text overlay. Owns the ScrollTrigger setup. Replaces the placeholder <section> in index.tsx.
ParticleField.tsx — r3f scene: camera, lights, the Points mesh. Accepts a stage prop (0–3) that drives which target positions are active.
useParticleTargets.ts — Hook. Computes the 4 sets of particle positions: scattered, fibonacci spiral, flower-of-life, sphere cluster. Memoized — calculated once on mount.
useHeroScroll.ts — Hook. Wraps GSAP ScrollTrigger scrub. Returns a 0–1 progress value that HeroSection uses to drive the stage transitions.
HeroFallback.tsx — Mobile fallback. Static SVG Flower of Life that fades in with the text. Shown when WebGL is unavailable or on touch devices.
