Hero Animation Trigger

The blueprint says particles coalesce "on scroll" — but how does the sequence feel before and during?

A

Idle drift → scroll morphs

On load: particles float gently in a slow, ambient drift. The ground is alive but formless. When user scrolls: particles coalesce → geometry → sphere → text. Scroll position drives the morph (scrub-linked).

Feels like: breathing void. The site is waiting for you.

B

Auto-play sequence → scroll to continue

On load: particles auto-animate through the full sequence (field → geometry → sphere → text) without any scroll. After text appears, scrolling continues to the next section. First-time cinematic feel.

Feels like: an opening title sequence. You watch, then navigate.

C

Completely still → first scroll triggers

On load: a completely static field. Truly silent ground — no motion at all until the user acts. First scroll pixel fires the entire sequence. Maximum contrast between stillness and movement.

Feels like: the site holds its breath until you arrive.
